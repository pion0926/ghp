Mission | Vision

Mission Statement
Global Civic Sharing promotes sustainable development and the self-reliance of communities by encouraging marginalized and vulnerable people worldwide.

Mission
  1. 1

    To promote community development through the capacity building of residents in developing countries

  2. 2

    To empower Global Civil Society

  3. 3

    To provide education and volunteer activities to address challenges facing humanity

  4. 4

    To eradicate poverty and achieve sustainable development through policy research and advocacy campaigns

  5. 5

    To expand the movement of Global Civic Sharing.

Core Values

  • People-Centered
    We consider people as our priority when planning the objectives of our actions

  • Respect for Diversity
    We take actions with all partners without any religious, racial, or political discrimination

  • Partnership
    We strengthen a collective effort with all partners that agree with poverty reduction and sustainable development


  • Lead for Change
    We constantly take on new challenges to lead and create change.
