Mission Statement

Global Civic Sharing promotes sustainable development and the self-reliance of communities by encouraging marginalized and vulnerable people worldwide.

Mission
  1. 1 To promote community development through the capacity building of residents in developing countries
  2. 2 To empower Global Civil Society
  3. 3 To provide education and volunteer activities to address
    challenges facing humanity
  4. 4 To eradicate poverty and achieve sustainable development through policy research and advocacy campaigns
  5. 5 To expand the movement of Global Civic Sharing.
